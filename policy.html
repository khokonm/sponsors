Web Lock Sponsorship Policy

For Sponsors:

  1. Content Submission: Sponsors must submit content (such as logos, banners, slogans) that align with the theme and values of our extension. Content should be suitable for a general audience and must not contain offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate material.
  2. Approval Process: Submitted content will undergo a review process to ensure it meets our guidelines. We reserve the right to reject or request modifications to content that do not meet our standards.
  3. Duration of Sponsorship: Sponsorship slots are allocated on a monthly basis. Sponsors must subscribe to our Buy Me a Coffee page to secure sponsorship for a specific month.
  4. Visibility: Sponsors will be featured on our extension in a dedicated sponsor section. The placement and visibility of sponsor content will be determined by our design and user experience considerations.
  5. Non-Transfer of User Data: We guarantee that no personal data of our users will be transferred to sponsors. Our commitment to user privacy is paramount, and we adhere to strict data protection policies.

For Users:

  1. Privacy Assurance: We prioritize the privacy and security of our users. No personal data, including browsing history or identifiable information, will be shared with sponsors or third parties for advertising purposes.
  2. Transparency: Users will be informed about sponsored content displayed on our extension. Sponsored materials will be clearly marked as such to maintain transparency.
  3. Opt-out Option: Users currently do not have an option to opt-out of viewing sponsored content within our extension settings, but we're working on it. In the future, we may offer an enhanced opt-out feature as a benefit for backers or contributors, where sponsored content may be removed for subscribed users.
  4. Feedback and Concerns: Users are encouraged to provide feedback or report any concerns regarding sponsored content. We value user input to maintain a positive and trustworthy environment.
  5. Continuous Improvement: We continuously review our sponsorship policies and practices to ensure they align with our commitment to user privacy and satisfaction.
